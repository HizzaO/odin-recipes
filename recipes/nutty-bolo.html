Spaghetti with nutty Bolo

Description

You like pasta but a normal bolognese is way to boring? Lucky you! The solution is right ahead. A tasty, nutty, vegan bolognese variant for the long-awaited variety in your pasta game

Ingredients

  • 500g vegan spaghetti
  • 2 red onions
  • 2 carrots
  • 100g celeriac
  • 2 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
  • 12 pitted kalamata olives
  • 100g walnut kernels
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 200ml vegtable broth
  • 2 cans of chunky tomatoes
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon of dried thyme
  • 1 teaspoon of sweet paprika powder
  • salt
  • black pepper
  • half a bunch of basil
