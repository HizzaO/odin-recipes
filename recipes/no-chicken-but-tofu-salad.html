No-Chicken-But-Tofu-Salad

Description

A delicious salad, without any chicken. But just as good. Or maybe even better? Perfect as a spread. Ideal with dark sourdough or mixed grain bread.

ingredients

Tofu-Salad

  • 400g of natural tofu
  • 1 tablespoon of extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon of roast chicken seasoning
  • 1 small can of madarin oranges
  • 1 small glass of champignons
  • half a bunch of chives

Dressing

  • 6 tablespoons vegan mayonnaise
  • 150g natural plant based yogurt (no sugar)
  • juice of half a lemon
  • 1 tablespoon vegan worecestershiresauce
  • 1 teaspoon curry powder
  • black pepper
