No-Chicken-But-Tofu-Salad

Description

A delicious salad, without any chicken. But just as good. Or maybe even better? Perfect as a spread. Ideal with dark sourdough or mixed grain bread.

ingredients

Tofu-Salad

  • 400g of natural tofu
  • 1 tablespoon of extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon of roast chicken seasoning
  • 1 small can of madarin oranges (reserve the juice)
  • 1 small glass of champignons
  • half a bunch of chives

Dressing

  • 6 tablespoons vegan mayonnaise
  • 150g natural plant based yogurt (no sugar)
  • juice of half a lemon
  • 1 tablespoon vegan worecestershiresauce
  • 1 teaspoon curry powder
  • black pepper

Steps

  1. Press excess liquid from the tofu with kitchen roll, then dice into small cubes of about 5mm
  2. heat the olive oil in a pan and fry the tofu cubes over medium heat for about 5 minutes, turning them frequently
  3. Sprinkle the Tofu with the fried chicken seasoning, stir, remove the pan from the heat, and allow to cool briefly
  4. Meanwhile, for the dressing, whisk together the mayonnaise, yogurt, lemon juice, worcestershire sauce and the curry powder in a large bowl until smooth. Add 4 tablespoons of the reserved mandarin juice and season generously with salt and pepper
  5. Cut the mandrins in half, slice the champignons as desired, add both to the dressing with the tofu and fold in. Wash the chives, chop finely and stir in
  6. Cover and let the tofu salad in the refrigerator for at least 60 minutes
  7. Season with salt and pepper to taste, mix briefly and serve as a spread
